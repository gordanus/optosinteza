Okviri

We are a wholesale optical company focusing on developing optical frames and sunglasses under the brand of Reserve. Reserve frames are characteristic for their modern design while focusing on high quality execution and every detail. When producing them we use only top – quality carefully selected materials. Reserve frames follow the latest fashion trends. The design is continuously developed and collection gradually supplemented with new models. Our goal is to provide our customers with a product in the good price/quality ratio. We are very good partner to our customers due to fast delivery of the goods and after-sale service. The brand is distributed in many European as well as non-European countries.
